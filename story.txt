Chapter 1: The Mysterious Missing Data
Once upon a time, there was a detective named Data who loved to solve puzzles...
Suddenly, a mysterious figure left behind a clue: a USB drive labeled "TOP SECRET".
Data opened it, only to find encrypted files that hinted at a hidden treasure...
Data worked tirelessly to decrypt the files
He finally managed to find all the pieces to the treasure map!
Data hurried to the coordinates the files gave him
Only to find his best friend Byte had beaten him to the punch!
